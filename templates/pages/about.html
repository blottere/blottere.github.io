Transitioning from an artist to a craftsman; a Character Animator to a UX Designer.

I am Pavithra Ramamurthy, a Masters in Human Computer Interaction Design Student at Indiana University. I worked as a character animator for nearly five years before I discovered UX.

As an animation artist I got to explore a wide range of human emotions and behaviour in the form of character acting and performance within the constraints of their surroundings. As a UX designer, I now dig deeper into the human behaviour in terms of their interactions with their surroudings!

I am looking for an amazing team of multifaceted designers who are just as curious and explorative; whom I can learn from and learn with as we build, innovate and design for humans and technology.
