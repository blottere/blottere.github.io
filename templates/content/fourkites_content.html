FourKites Website Visual Design

FourKites

FourKites is a logistics service provider in Chicago, IL, that enables real-time tracking between shippers and truck companies. They have their unique brand identity and client base. My job was to design the blog pages, the jobs section, and the contact page while ensuring consistency in the visual design throughout the website.

Blog Page

The blog pages match the visual style of the landing page. The blog categories are displayed above the posts for easy filtering. Navigation between older and newer posts are placed at the bottom of the page for ease of transition to the next content once the post is read.

What I Learned

  • It was intersting to work within the contraints of an existing visual style. I got to experiment with the design of the pages while utilising the visual elements of rest of the website.
  • The constraint helped me understand how to compose unique layouts even within the bounds of an existing style of the brand.
